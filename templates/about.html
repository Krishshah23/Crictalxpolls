🏏 About Us

CRICTALX POLLS

Where Cricket Meets Prediction

OUR VISION

Built by cricket enthusiasts, for cricket enthusiasts. We created Crictalx Polls to bring an exciting, competitive edge to watching IPL matches. Predict, compete, and celebrate every boundary and wicket with fellow fans.

HOW IT WORKS

  • Predict: Vote on match polls before each game starts. Choose your option and confidence level.
  • Earn Points: Get rewarded for correct predictions. Accuracy builds your leaderboard ranking.
  • Power Up: Spin the daily roulette for boosts. Apply multipliers to amplify your points on any poll.
  • Compete: Climb the weekly MVP spotlight and keep your streak alive across the season.
  • Celebrate: Win trophies, earn badges, and own your cricket fandom.

THE ROULETTE

Every player gets one free spin per 24 hours. Land on multipliers (2×, 3×, 4×, 5×), defensive shields, chaotic wildcards, or special utility rewards. Each boost lasts 7 days and can be applied to any poll before it expires. Stack multiple active boosts in your inventory - manage them wisely!

WHY PLAY?

  • 🏆 Compete against friends and climb the rankings
  • 🎰 Daily roulette spins bring fresh strategic choices
  • ⚡ Boosts add risk, reward, and comeback mechanics
  • 🎯 Earn trophies and badges for achievements
  • 📊 Track your accuracy, streaks, and stats over time
